# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Everyone from designers and teachers to students and casual users — people who need a low-friction, browser-based canvas for sketching, collaborating, teaching, or sharing quick visual ideas.

## Product Purpose

Draw-It is a lightweight, collaborative drawing tool that enables users to create, share, and iterate on visual ideas in real time. Success is measured by fast time-to-first-canvas, seamless realtime collaboration, and easy sharing.

## Brand Personality

Minimal; Creative; Friendly

## Anti-references

- Generic purple-gradient marketing sites
- Overly tacky or noisy visual styles

## Design Principles

- Keep interactions low-friction: minimize steps to draw, share, and invite collaborators.
- Prioritize accessibility and inclusivity (WCAG 2.1 AA target).
- Use a restrained, minimal visual language; emphasize clarity of affordances over decoration.
- Make collaboration obvious and discoverable: presence, cursors, and simple share flows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Support keyboard interactions, clear focus states, sufficient color contrast, and reduced-motion preferences.
